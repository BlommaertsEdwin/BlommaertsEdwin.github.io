I created a Tree!

January 13, 2022 One-minute read
me
game dev • Arena

I was tired of seeing my little man running around stark naked without anywhere to hide behind. So I created a tree for him. Using Y-sort, collision shapes and static bodies I made it possible for him to go behind the tree but not go through it.
