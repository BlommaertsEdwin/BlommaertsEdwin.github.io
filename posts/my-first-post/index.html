The first blog post

October 25, 2015

This is the first blog post of many to come. This will be my personal space on the vast internet. Most likely I will be writing about programming and board games.

My interest in board games started when I was young and for SinterKlaas I asked my parents for a copy of Hero Quest. I remember coming home and seeing the box, I tore it open and pushed the cardboard prints out of the frames, twisted and turned the models to get them out of the plastic sprues and assembled the furniture and doors.

My interest in programming started when I was in school, somehow, it felt naturally to me, moulding code into a process that would give you the exact behaviour that you wanted. My confidence got boosted when a lecturer introduced me to Object Oriented programming and asked my to show the work I had done on a fully object oriented Stop Watch. I shared the code with fellow students and they used the objects in their own implementation of the stopwatch project.

Another fun project I did when I was younger, was coding up a Sudoku solver. Although it was a very procedural script, it was fun to solve puzzles in a matter of seconds by applying brute force and recursion methods.
